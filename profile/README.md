# Display Fusion

**Display Fusion** is a powerful, flexible multi‑monitor management solution designed to help professionals, gamers, and creative teams control multiple displays, organize windows, and personalize every screen.  
With **Display Fusion**, you can turn scattered monitors into one unified workspace.  
**Display Fusion** supports per‑monitor taskbars, custom wallpapers, window snapping, saved profiles, and hotkeys – making it essential for anyone with two or more screens.

---

## About Display Fusion

**Display Fusion** combines the simplicity of display settings with the power of a professional window manager. Unlike basic OS tools, **Display Fusion** offers deep customization: different wallpapers per monitor, independent taskbars, custom split zones, and trigger‑based profiles.  
**Display Fusion** works seamlessly with any multi‑monitor setup (laptop + external, ultrawide, portrait, mixed DPI) and provides real‑time profile switching.  
Whether you manage a trading desk, a video editing suite, or a coding setup, **Display Fusion** adapts to your workflow, not the other way around.  
**Display Fusion** respects your privacy – all settings stay local with optional cloud backup for profiles.  
With **Display Fusion**, you gain clarity, reduce window clutter, and complete more tasks across all screens.

## Key Features of Display Fusion

- **Per‑Monitor Taskbars** – **Display Fusion** adds a dedicated taskbar to each screen with only that monitor’s apps.  
- **Custom Wallpapers** – **Display Fusion** sets different images or slideshows per display.  
- **Window Snapping** – **Display Fusion** lets you snap windows to zones, edges, or grid cells.  
- **Monitor Profiles** – Save and switch layouts instantly inside **Display Fusion** (home/office/presentation).  
- **Hotkeys & Triggers** – Control **Display Fusion** with global shortcuts or automatic events.  
- **Split Screen Zones** – Divide any monitor into virtual zones using **Display Fusion**.  
- **Multi‑Monitor Wallpaper Management** – Stretch, tile, or span images across screens with **Display Fusion**.  
- **Fade to Desktop** – Quickly hide all windows via **Display Fusion** to see wallpapers.  
- **Window Position Profiles** – Save app positions and restore them with **Display Fusion**.  
- **Command Line Control** – Automate **Display Fusion** actions from scripts or launchers.  
- **Multi‑Monitor Screensavers** – Use **Display Fusion** to set unique screensavers per display.  
- **Monitor Flipping / Rotation** – Adjust orientation directly inside **Display Fusion**.  
- **DPI Awareness** – **Display Fusion** handles mixed DPI and scaling differences.  
- **Portrait & Ultrawide Support** – Full support in **Display Fusion** for vertical and 21:9 displays.  
- **Backup & Restore** – Export your **Display Fusion** settings to JSON or cloud.
